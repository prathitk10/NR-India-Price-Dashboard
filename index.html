📉 Moving Averages — MA7 & MA20

Moving Average Rules

  • MA-7 = 7-day average. Reacts quickly to recent moves.
  • MA-20 = 20-day average. Shows medium-term trend.
  • Golden Cross — MA7 crosses above MA20 → Bullish, consider buying
  • Death Cross — MA7 crosses below MA20 → Bearish, reduce buying
  • Price above both MAs → healthy uptrend
  • Price below both MAs → downtrend established
  • Dips to MA-20 in uptrend = buying opportunities

📐 Bollinger Bands (20D, ±2σ)

Bollinger Band Rules

  • Band Squeeze (narrow bands) → Big move imminent, watch direction
  • Price near upper band → Stretched high, risk of pullback
  • Price near lower band → Stretched low, buying opportunity
  • Bounce off lower band → Strong buy signal
  • Middle band (MA-20) = support in uptrend, resistance in downtrend
  • Confirm with RSI — lower band + RSI below 30 = very strong buy

⚡ RSI — Relative Strength Index (14-day)

RSI Rules

  • Above 70 — Overbought → Prices risen too fast, correction likely
  • Below 30 — Oversold → Prices fallen too fast, bounce likely
  • RSI crossing back below 70 → Rally losing steam, reduce exposure
  • RSI crossing back above 30 → Worst of fall over, start accumulating
  • RSI above 70 + near upper Bollinger Band = strong caution signal
  • RSI below 30 + near lower Bollinger Band = strong buying signal
  • RSI 30–70 = neutral, follow trend direction

🚀 30-Day Rate of Change (ROC)

ROC Rules

  • ROC above zero → Price higher than 30 days ago, upward momentum
  • ROC below zero → Price lower than 30 days ago, downward momentum
  • ROC crossing zero upward → Momentum shifted bullish, buy signal
  • ROC crossing zero downward → Momentum shifted bearish, caution
  • ROC above +5% → Rising fast, risk of being overbought
  • ROC below −5% → Fallen fast, potential buying opportunity
  • Divergence — Price new high but ROC lower high → Reversal warning
